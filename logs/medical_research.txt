- A healthy breakfast for diabetics should focus on high-fiber, low-glycemic index (GI) foods to help manage blood sugar levels.
- Suggested Indian Breakfast Options for Diabetics:
  - Vegetable Daliya (Broken Wheat Porridge)
  - Moong Dal Chilla (Savory Pancakes)
  - Besan Chilla (Gram Flour Pancakes)
  - Vegetable Oats Upma
  - Sprouted Salad
  - Idli/Sambar
  - Vegetable Poha
- According to the American Diabetes Association, a diet rich in high-fiber foods such as vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and legumes can help improve blood sugar control and reduce the risk of heart disease.
- Poorly managed breakfast choices can lead to spikes in blood sugar levels, contributing to long-term complications such as nerve damage, kidney disease, and cardiovascular issues.
- Potential Complications:
  - Hyperglycemia
  - Hypoglycemia
  - Weight Gain
